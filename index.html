About Heritedge

Preserving Sikkim's spiritual heritage for future generations

Our Mission

Heritedge aims to preserve and promote Sikkim's rich Buddhist heritage through digital innovation. Our platform serves as a bridge between ancient wisdom and modern technology, making the spiritual treasures of Sikkim's monasteries accessible to people worldwide.

Our Vision

To create a comprehensive digital repository of Sikkim's monastic heritage, fostering cultural understanding and supporting sustainable tourism that benefits local communities.

Cultural Significance

Sikkim, known as Beyul Demozong (the hidden sacred land), has been blessed by Guru Padmasambhava and serves as a spiritual sanctuary. With over 200 monasteries representing various Buddhist sects, Sikkim stands as a beacon of Buddhist learning and practice in the Himalayas.
